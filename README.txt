Apart from tracking speed, distance and elevation of the runner, this application uses the formula described at page 7 of the United States Patend by the number 6,013,099 for the "Walking/Running Heart Rate Monitoring System" by Kip Michael Karkanen. Simply insert your weight at the appropriate textbox, in Kg, and state the type of the excersise, ticking the appropriate. At any time, you can commence the recording of the personal performance, by taping the button to set the starting point from the spot that you're standing.
